SAFEKEEPING OF THE RECORD OF PROCEEDINGS

1. All documents and recordings, pertaining to a disciplinary matter, will be kept in safe custody by
the Registrar. A student1 is, however, entitled to receive copies of such documents and transcription of
recordings at his/her own expense.

2. Such documents and transcription of recordings will be held by the University for a period of three years
after a matter has been finalised.
